What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the basic functionality of our website, such as user authentication, video players, shopping carts, and security features.
  • Prefrences Cookies: Help us remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience on our Site.
  • Statistics Cookies: Allow us to understand how visitors interact with our Site so we can improve its performance and features.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements based on your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in these ways:

  • Consent Pop-Up: You can change or withdraw your consent anytime at the bottom of this page or using the Cookiebot consent pop-up icon shown at the bottom off all pages of our Site.
  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or alert you when cookies are being sent.
  • Opt-Out Links: Many third-party advertising networks offer opt-out options. To learn more, visit organizations such as the Digital Advertising Alliance, the Network Advertising Initiative, or similar self-regulatory programs available in your region.
  • If you specifically do not like to being tracked using analytics tools such as Google Analytics you can learn how Google uses data and find opt-out options.
